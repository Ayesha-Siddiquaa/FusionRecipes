Kadhai Chicken

Kadhai chicken is a popular North Indian dish known for its bold flavors and rich gravy. It's prepared by cooking succulent chicken pieces with an aromatic blend of spices and tomatoes in a traditional kadhai (Indian wok). The dish is characterized by its vibrant red color and robust taste, making it a favorite among spice enthusiasts. Kadhai chicken is often served with naan, roti, or steamed rice, making it a satisfying meal for lunch or dinner.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Non-Veg Chicken

instructions

Marinate Chicken:

Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.

Prepare Spice Blend:

Combine coriander, cumin, garam masala, and kasuri methi.

Saut√© Aromatics:

Cook onions, green chilies, and bell peppers until golden brown.

Add Spices and Tomatoes:

Stir in spice blend, then add tomatoes and cook until softened.

Cook Chicken:

Add marinated chicken, cook until partially done.

Simmer Garnish and Serve:

Garnish with coriander leaves and cream. Serve hot with naan or rice. Adjust spices to taste. Enjoy your flavorful kadhai chicken!

ingredients

500 grams chicken, bone-in pieces

2 onions, finely chopped

2 tomatoes, chopped

2 green chilies, slit

1 bell pepper (capsicum), diced

1/4 cup yogurt

2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 tablespoon dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)

Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Salt to taste

Spices:

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 tablespoon coriander powder

1 teaspoon cumin powder

1 teaspoon garam masala

1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Pinch of asafoetida (hing)
