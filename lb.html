Lucknowi Biryani

Lucknowi Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish originating from Lucknow, India. It is known for its rich aroma, tender meat, and aromatic spices. The dish is a delightful blend of long-grained Basmati rice, marinated meat (typically chicken or lamb), and a medley of spices. Cooked with saffron-infused milk, fried onions, and fragrant rose water, Lucknowi Biryani offers a unique and indulgent dining experience.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Rice Chicken Non-Veg

instructions

Step 1:

Marinate meat with yogurt and spices.

Step 2:

Saut√© onions and spices in ghee.

Step 3:

Cook marinated meat until tender.

Step 4:

Parboil Basmati rice with spices.

Step 5:

Layer rice and meat in a pan.

Step 6:

Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, and mint leaves between layers.

Step 7:

Cook covered until rice is fully done.

Step 8:

Fluff and serve hot.

ingredients

500 grams of meat (chicken, lamb, or goat)

2 cups of Basmati rice

1 cup of plain yogurt

2 large onions, thinly sliced

4 cloves of garlic, minced

1-inch piece of ginger, grated

2-3 green chilies, chopped

1/2 cup of chopped fresh mint leaves

1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro (coriander) leaves

1/4 cup of ghee or vegetable oil

Whole spices: bay leaves, cinnamon sticks, cloves, cardamom pods

Ground spices: turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder

Saffron threads soaked in warm milk

Salt to taste
