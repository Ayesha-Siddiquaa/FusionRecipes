Mysore Pak

Mysore Pak is a delectable South Indian sweet renowned for its rich taste and melt-in-your-mouth texture. Originating from the city of Mysore in Karnataka, this indulgent treat is made from a few simple ingredients like gram flour (besan), sugar, ghee, and cardamom. The result is a delightful combination of sweetness and nuttiness that leaves a lasting impression on your taste buds. Traditionally enjoyed during festivals and special occasions, Mysore Pak is a timeless classic that continues to captivate dessert lovers worldwide.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : veg Desert

instructions

Prepare Ingredients:

Gather gram flour (besan), sugar, ghee, and cardamom.

Roast Gram Flour:

Heat ghee in a pan, add gram flour, and roast until aromatic.

Make Sugar Syrup:

Heat sugar and water to make a syrup, then bring it to a gentle boil.

Combine Ingredients:

Mix the sugar syrup into the roasted gram flour, stirring continuously.

Add Cardamom:

Crush and add cardamom seeds for flavor. Transfer and Set.

Serve or Store:

Serve warm or store cooled pieces in an airtight container. Enjoy the delicious Mysore Pak!

ingredients

1 cup gram flour (besan)

1 cup sugar

1 ½ cups ghee

1 teaspoon cardamom seeds (crushed)

½ cup water
